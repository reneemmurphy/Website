# Adoption Research

A working space for notes, sources, and questions related to the child/family adoption process.

## Purpose

This directory collects research material to help understand and navigate child/family adoption. It is a personal knowledge base — not legal advice.

## Scope

Topics that may be explored here include:

- Types of adoption (domestic, international, foster-to-adopt, kinship, private)
- Agencies, attorneys, and licensed professionals
- Home study and licensing requirements
- Legal process and timelines
- Costs, grants, and financial assistance
- Post-placement and post-adoption support
- Open vs. closed adoption considerations
- Relevant books, articles, and first-person accounts

## How to use this folder

Add notes, links, and source material here as the research progresses. Keep file names descriptive (e.g. `home-study-overview.md`, `agency-comparison.md`) so the folder stays easy to navigate.

## Disclaimer

Nothing in this folder is legal, medical, or professional advice. Always consult a licensed adoption attorney or agency for guidance specific to your situation.
